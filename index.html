DALE CHRISTOPHER WELLS

Artist • Curator • Gallerist

About

Dale Christopher Wells is a contemporary British artist whose practice explores history, memory, place and social narratives through drawing, painting, installation and research-led projects.


A professional artist, curator and gallery director, Dale co-directed Turntable Gallery in Grimsby for four years and has exhibited widely throughout the United Kingdom. His work forms part of the collection of Manchester Art Gallery and has been featured in numerous exhibitions, public projects and cultural initiatives.


His practice often combines historical research with contemporary visual language, creating works that engage audiences through both artistic and educational experiences.
